Why Early Detection Matters

Citrus diseases can devastate entire orchards, leading to significant economic losses for farmers. Identifying a disease in its early stages is the most critical step toward managing its spread and minimizing damage. This tool is designed to provide a quick and accessible analysis of citrus leaves, helping you make informed decisions to protect your crops. By simply uploading an image, you can get an instant classification of potential threats.
